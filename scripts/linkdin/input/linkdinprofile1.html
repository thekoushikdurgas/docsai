About

Hailing from Hyderabad, India, Anirudh brings over a decade of recruitment expertise across tech and non-tech domains. I have led hiring for powerhouses like Salesforce, Amazon, Livspace, and KPMG—driving end-to-end recruitment strategies from early-career to director-level roles. My track record is impressive: 350+ hires in a year, 12X revenue growth, 35%+ diversity hiring quarter after quarter, and leading multi-city drives/hackathons with 200+ candidates. I am not just a recruiter—I'm a strategic advisor, a data-led hiring champion, and someone who thrives on building inclusive, high-performing teams. Whether it’s transforming hiring frameworks, optimizing cost per hire, or launching employer branding campaigns that attract 10,000+ engagement, I know how to move the hiring needle with purpose. Outside of work, I am an avid trekker, an unapologetic pet-lover, and a curious explorer of conspiracy theories and the paranormal (ask him about Cult Horror Films, Moon landing conspiracies or Area 51 —you’ve been warned!). I collect antiques and watches from around the world.

    🌏 Bengaluru, India
    🕘 Full time (hybrid)
    💼 14-18 years (5+ years in heading global teams / enterprise level projects)
    🧑‍💻 Machine Learning Experience Team
