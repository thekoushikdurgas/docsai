About the Opportunity:


The organization offers an AI-native platform for Financial Planning & Analysis (FP&A), addressing challenges finance teams face with scattered data, multiple systems, and time-consuming spreadsheet management. The role focuses on improving product design and frontend development to enhance user experience and operational efficiency. The team hires remotely across the Americas.



Responsibilities:


• Drive product look and feel by standardizing layouts and UI patterns

• Build performant user interfaces that manage complex data and operations

• Deliver pixel-perfect, user-friendly designs

• Increase development velocity through component architecture and best practices

• Own the frontend experience end-to-end



Requirements:


• Strong frontend fundamentals with deep React and TypeScript experience

• Familiarity with Next.js and Vercel

• Background as a founder, engineering lead, or startup builder with a proactive mindset

• Ability to select simple and effective tools for implementation

• Commitment to pixel-perfect design and user experience

• Clear and persuasive communication with technical and non-technical audiences

• Demonstrated growth and operating above current level

• Strong design sensibility and attention to detail

• Experience building high-performance UIs that remain fast under load

• Experience developing complex B2B products, ideally from scratch



Benefits & Perks:


• Remote work opportunity focused on candidates within the Americas
